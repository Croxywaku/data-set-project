This project presents a comprehensive analysis of customer behavior, revenue performance, and churn patterns using Power BI. The dashboard provides actionable insights into customer distribution, device usage, revenue trends, and key factors influencing churn.
Analyze customer demographics and behavior
 • Track revenue performance across regions and products
 • Identify key drivers of customer churn
 • Provide insights to improve customer retention and business growth
 Key Insights
 • Majority of customers are retained, but churn is significantly influenced by pricing and network quality
 • 5G broadband routers generate the highest revenue
 • Certain regions and states contribute disproportionately to data usage
 • Customer satisfaction (rating) is relatively low, indicating room for improvement
 Churn Analysis
 • Churn rate visualization
 • Key reasons for churn:
 • High call tariffs
 • Poor network
 • Costly data plans
 • Poor customer service
 • Customer churn distribution
